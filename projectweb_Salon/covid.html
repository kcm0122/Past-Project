COVID-19 protection measures

Wear Mask

Our employee will wear their mask all day when they are working. After 4 hours period, the old masks will be thrown and wear new masks. Ensure their masks will not become invalid.

3-dose Vaccine Pass

All the employee has done 3 doses of the vaccine. So that they pass the government requirement. Ensure customers enjoy our service in a protected environment.

Covid-test

Our employee is required to do self-covid test everyday. If they are positive after the test, our salon will not allow them to come to our salon until they are negative and after 14 days.
